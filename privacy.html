Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Outfit Mirror AI ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can contact us regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Photos and User Content

The app may collect and process content you choose to upload, including:

  • Clothing photos
  • Appearance photos used for See On Me previews
  • Outfit photos submitted for AI analysis
  • Saved looks and styling preferences

These images are used to provide app features such as wardrobe management, outfit recommendations, AI outfit review, and See On Me previews.

Usage Information

We may collect limited app usage information, such as:

  • Feature usage counts
  • Premium or trial status
  • AI usage limits and abuse-prevention data
  • App interaction and diagnostic information

Device and Technical Information

We may collect limited technical data needed to operate, secure, and improve the app, such as device type, operating system, app version, error logs, and performance information.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide wardrobe and outfit recommendation features
  • Generate AI-powered styling feedback and previews
  • Improve app quality, reliability, and performance
  • Manage Premium subscriptions, trials, and feature access
  • Prevent misuse, abuse, excessive automated usage, and fraud
  • Respond to support and privacy requests

3. AI Processing

Some features of Outfit Mirror AI use third-party artificial intelligence providers to analyze uploaded content and generate styling recommendations or outfit previews.

Uploaded photos and related metadata may be securely sent to these providers only for the purpose of delivering the requested app functionality.

AI-generated results may not always be accurate and are provided for styling and informational purposes only.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play.

We do not collect, receive, or store your payment card details.

We may store subscription status, trial status, subscription type, and related entitlement information to unlock Premium features.

5. Diagnostics and Error Monitoring

We may use monitoring tools, such as error tracking and diagnostic services, to identify crashes, bugs, performance problems, and service failures.

Diagnostic data may include technical information such as app version, device type, operating system, error messages, and limited usage context.

We do not intentionally send payment card data, raw API keys, or unnecessary sensitive information to diagnostic systems.

6. Advertising

The free version of the app may display advertisements.

Advertising partners may collect limited information to display, measure, or improve ads according to their own privacy policies.

Premium users may receive an ad-free experience depending on the subscription plan.

7. Data Storage and Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide app functionality, improve the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and prevent abuse.

Some uploaded or generated content may be stored to support features such as wardrobe management and saved looks, unless deleted or no longer needed.

8. Data Sharing

We may share limited data with service providers that help operate the app, including:

  • AI processing providers
  • Cloud hosting or storage providers
  • Crash reporting and diagnostics providers
  • Payment platform providers such as Apple App Store and Google Play
  • Advertising partners for the free version of the app

We do not sell your uploaded photos or personal content.

9. User Choices and Data Deletion
